Our Story

To make world-class tech education accessible to every Pakistani student — regardless of background or budget.

Our Mission

Building the Next Generation of Tech Leaders

Founded in 2023 in Rawalpindi, SkillSpark was born out of frustration. Our founders couldn't find affordable, practical, project-based tech education that matched what companies actually needed.

So we built it ourselves. Today, we serve over 5,200 students across Pakistan with curriculum designed in partnership with hiring managers at leading tech companies.

Our Journey

From Idea to Impact

In just 2 years, SkillSpark has grown from a weekend project in a Rawalpindi apartment to Pakistan's most trusted tech learning platform.

January 2023
The Idea is Born

Ali and Sara, frustrated by the lack of practical coding education, begin drafting the first SkillSpark curriculum on a Google Doc.

May 2023
First Cohort Launched

SkillSpark's beta launch with 40 students in Rawalpindi. 100% hands-on, project-based. First student lands a job in week 10.

October 2023
Platform Goes Online

Full web platform launches. First 500 students enrolled within 30 days. Waitlist grows to 2,000+.

March 2024
AI & Data Science Track Added

Most requested track goes live. 300 students enrolled in the first week. Partnership with 3 AI companies for job placement.

2025 – Today
5,200+ Students & Growing

SkillSpark now serves students across Pakistan. 89% job placement rate. Expanding to 10 more cities in 2025.
